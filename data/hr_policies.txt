ACME CORP — HR POLICIES KNOWLEDGE BASE
=======================================

1. EQUIPMENT POLICY
───────────────────
- All new hires receive a company laptop within 3 business days of start date
- Remote employees receive a home office allowance of $500 (first year only)
- Engineers (Software, Data, DevOps) receive: laptop + dual monitors + mechanical keyboard
- Non-engineers receive: laptop + single monitor
- Equipment requests must be submitted via IT ticket system 1 week before start date

2. SYSTEM ACCESS POLICY
────────────────────────
- Corporate email (Google Workspace) must be created on Day 1 before 9 AM
- Slack workspace access granted on Day 1
- Role-specific tools granted within 2 business days
- Admin/elevated access requires: manager approval + security team review (5-day process)
- All access follows principle of least privilege

3. LEGAL & COMPLIANCE
──────────────────────
- NDA must be signed BEFORE first day of employment
- Background check must be completed and cleared before Day 1
- All employees must complete mandatory compliance training within 30 days
- Data privacy training required within 14 days
- Security awareness training required within 7 days

4. BENEFITS ENROLLMENT
───────────────────────
- Health insurance: enrollment window is 30 days from start date
- Dental & Vision: same 30-day window
- 401k enrollment: available immediately, employer matches up to 4%
- Life insurance: automatic enrollment Day 1
- PTO accrual: begins from Day 1 (15 days/year for first 2 years)

5. ONBOARDING TIMELINE
───────────────────────
Week 1 (Days 1-5):
  - IT setup and account creation
  - HR orientation (benefits, policies, culture)
  - Meet with direct manager
  - Team introductions

Week 2-4 (Days 6-30):
  - Department-specific onboarding
  - Complete all mandatory training
  - Begin role-specific work with mentor/buddy
  - Benefits enrollment deadline

Day 30: Benefits enrollment deadline
Day 90: First performance check-in with manager
Day 180: 6-month review

6. REMOTE WORK POLICY
──────────────────────
- Hybrid roles: minimum 2 days/week in office
- Remote roles: quarterly in-person team sessions (travel covered)
- Home office ergonomics assessment provided for fully remote employees
- VPN required for all remote access to internal systems

7. PROBATION PERIOD
────────────────────
- Standard probation period: 90 days
- During probation: monthly check-ins with manager
- Confirmation of employment after successful 90-day review
- Benefits active from Day 1 regardless of probation status

8. EMERGENCY CONTACTS & ESCALATION
────────────────────────────────────
- HR queries: hr@acmecorp.com
- IT support: itsupport@acmecorp.com (tickets via portal)
- Manager escalations: through Band rooms or direct manager contact
- Legal/compliance questions: legal@acmecorp.com
